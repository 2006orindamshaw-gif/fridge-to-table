# 🥗 Fridge to Table: AI-Powered Recipe Discovery

**Fridge to Table** is a modern, full-stack web application designed to eliminate food waste and answer the age-old question: *"What's for dinner?"* 

By leveraging cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence, this app allows users to simply snap a photo of their ingredients and instantly receive personalized recipe suggestions tailored to what they actually have in their kitchen.

## ✨ Key Features

*   **📸 AI Fridge Scanner**: Powered by **Google Gemini Vision API**, users can upload photos of their fridge or pantry. The AI identifies raw ingredients with high precision, removing the need for manual typing.
*   **🍳 Intelligent Recipe Search**: Integrates with the **Spoonacular API** to find thousands of recipes filtered by the ingredients you have on hand.
*   **🛒 Dynamic Shopping List**: Missing a few items for that perfect meal? Add them to your integrated shopping list with a single click.
*   **🗄️ Digital Pantry**: Keep track of your staples and long-term ingredients to ensure they never go to waste.
*   *💖 Favorite Recipes**: Save your favorite discoveries to a personal collection for quick access later.
*   **🥗 Dietary Filters**: Support for various diets including Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, Ketogenic, and more.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **Frontend**: React.js, Material UI (MUI), Vite, Axios
*   **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
*   **Database**: MongoDB (Mongoose)
*   **AI Engine**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
*   **Recipe Data**: Spoonacular API

## 🚀 Vision & Goal

In an era where sustainability matters more than ever, **Fridge to Table** empowers home cooks to be more resourceful. By turning the ingredients you already own into delicious meals, we help reduce food waste, save money, and make cooking an effortless, creative experience.
